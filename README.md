# Indian Knowledge System (IKS) — Interactive Study Guide

A comprehensive, high-quality interactive study guide for **AMC 1001**, covering the vast 5000-year history of the Indian Knowledge System.

## 🌟 Features

- **Multi-Chapter Learning**: 6 deep-dive chapters from the Vedic Corpus to Ancient Number Systems.
- **Interactive Modals**: Detailed explanations, facts, and cross-references for every topic.
- **Searchable Database**: Quickly find any concept, term, or historical figure.
- **Quick Quiz**: Test your knowledge with interactive checks.
- **Synthesis Section**: See how different branches of knowledge interconnect.
- **Premium Design**: A "liquid glass" aesthetic with smooth transitions and responsive layouts.

## 📖 Content Overview

1. **Chapter 1**: Foundation & Classification of IKS
2. **Chapter 2**: The Vedic Corpus & Vedangas
3. **Chapter 3**: Philosophical Systems (Vedic & Non-Vedic)
4. **Chapter 4**: Wisdom through Puranas, Epics & Niti-Sastras
5. **Chapter 5**: Linguistics, Panini & the Science of Sound
6. **Chapter 6**: The Indian Number System & Historical Evidence

## 🛠 Built With

- **HTML5** (Semantic structure)
- **Vanilla CSS3** (Custom properties, Glassmorphism, Animations)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (Client-side routing, Search logic, Modal system)
- **Google Fonts** (Playfair Display, DM Sans, DM Mono)
